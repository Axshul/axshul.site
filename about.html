Hey, I'm Anshul Namdev. I'm a Technical Architect and AI Engineer originally from Shivpuri, currently operating out of Bhopal.

My path into engineering wasn't a standard one. It started extremely early, in the 6th grade, when I first touched programming. Since those initial experiments, I've spent years obsessing over the mechanics of high-performance code and scalable systems.

Lately, my focus has shifted heavily into the world of AI and workflow automation. I've become an expert in n8n. This journey actually began by observing my brother Deepankar Bhadrasen. He's currently the Founding AI Engineer at TrueHorizonAI. Seeing the potential of the systems he was building inspired me to push the boundaries of what's possible with automated AI agents.

I don't just build scripts; I architect entire ecosystems. I've successfully deployed multiple complex n8n automation projects that handle everything from data orchestration to autonomous AI decision-making.

Professionally, I've had the privilege of holding high-impact roles. I've worked with FRVR as their Technical Architect, where I helped shape the structural integrity of their platforms. Prior to that, I served as the Principal Software Engineer for the BlueWizard Digital project, leading engineering efforts for premium digital experiences.

My technical arsenal is broad but deep: Java, JavaScript, Python, Bash, and Ruby. Whether it's crafting a high-performance backend with Spring Boot or building a slick interface with the MERN stack, I build with precision and scale in mind.

Beyond the daily grind of architecture, I'm passionate about sharing what I know. My current mission is to climb the ranks and become the top writer at CodeProb, where I document my technical deep-dives and architectural findings for the next generation of engineers.
