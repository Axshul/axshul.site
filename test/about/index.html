Hey, I'm Anshul Namdev. I'm a Technical Architect, AI Engineer, n8n Community Moderator, and AI Automation Specialist available for contract work. Originally from Shivpuri, currently operating out of Bhopal. My path into engineering started in the 6th grade, and I've spent every year since obsessing over the mechanics of high-performance code and scalable systems.

Origin Story

My journey into AI and workflow automation was sparked by observing my brother Deepankar Bhadrasen, currently the Founding AI Engineer at TrueHorizonAI. Seeing the potential of the systems he was building inspired me to push the boundaries of what's possible with automated AI agents.

I don't just build scripts - I architect entire ecosystems. I've successfully deployed multiple complex n8n automation projects that handle everything from data orchestration to autonomous AI decision-making.

Experience

I've had the privilege of holding high-impact roles. I've worked with FRVR as their Technical Architect, where I helped shape the structural integrity of their platforms. Prior to that, I served as the Principal Software Engineer for the BlueWizard Digital project, leading engineering efforts for premium digital experiences.

Most recently, I co-founded Qixazow.com - a joint venture with two fellow AI professionals. Qixazow is a publication and AI consultancy built for people who want to stay ahead of the curve. We write in-depth articles and produce video content covering n8n, Claude, and the tools shaping the future of AI.
